01
20–29
Foundation

Build the base

This is where discipline is born. The habits you set now will carry you for decades.

Diet Learn whole foods. High protein. No steroids, no junk. Establish the eating pattern early.
Exercise Build muscle mass & cardiovascular base. Train consistently — this is when your body is most adaptable.
Fasting Practice occasional fasting. Learn to listen to your body's signals of hunger vs habit.
Mindset Dream big. Learn from failure. As Khan says: "Compromise for your dream, but never on your dream."
02
30–39
Strengthen

Build density & consistency

Career and family pull your attention. Consistency is the only thing that will carry you through.

Diet Master the meal structure: heavy lunch, light dinner. Add superfoods — honey, chia, walnuts.
Exercise Compound movements are critical. Build bone density now — it only gets harder after 40.
Fasting Introduce regular fasting windows. Your metabolism still adapts quickly at this age.
Mindset Handle pressure. Develop mental toughness. Routine is your anchor — protect it fiercely.
03
40–49
Maintain

Prevent decline

Testosterone naturally declines. Metabolic rate slows. Your discipline must increase to compensate.

Diet Light dinners become non-negotiable. Portion control matters more. Prioritize protein at every meal.
Exercise Weight resistance is essential for maintaining muscle. Add the exercise bike for joint-friendly cardio.
Fasting Regular fasting becomes a powerful tool for autophagy and metabolic reset.
Mindset Deepen your reading habit. Mental stimulation prevents cognitive decline. Sleep early, rise early.
04
50–59
Preserve

Fight entropy

This is where most men give up and accept decline. You will not. Every habit now is an investment in your 70s.

Diet Fasting is essential. Superfoods daily. Your digestive system needs the rest that comes from light dinners.
Exercise Weight resistance is non-negotiable for bone density. Bike for heart health. Move every single day.
Fasting Autophagy becomes your ally against inflammation and cellular aging. Fast consistently.
Mindset Avoid comfort-seeking. As Khan says, "The human body is made to struggle." Read, reflect, stay curious.
05
60–69
Sustain

Adapt but never stop

Your body changes. Your approach adapts. But the discipline does not waver.

Diet Whole foods only. Natural, locally sourced. Superfoods are your medicine. No processed food ever.
Exercise Adapted but non-negotiable. Barbell, light weights, bike. Movement every single day without exception.
Fasting Fasting is your primary tool for cellular maintenance. Your body thrives under controlled stress.
Mindset Daily mental routine is critical. Quran, books, newspapers. A sharp mind requires daily exercise.
06
70+
Thrive

Imran Khan — living proof

This is what it looks like when a lifetime of discipline compounds. At 73, incarcerated, with limited space — still training, still fasting, still reading, still unstoppable.

Diet Porridge, dates, coffee. Heavy lunch. Light dinner of fruit & milk. Superfoods daily.
Exercise Daily. Exercise bike, barbell, 9kg weights. Compound movements for muscle, bone & testosterone.
Fasting Regular fasting for autophagy. Every organ works harder, functions better under stress.
Mindset "Never give up." Reads Quran daily. Newspapers. Books. Wakes early. Sleeps early. Never seeks comfort.

"Never give up, no matter how hard life gets. Pain will eventually subside."
